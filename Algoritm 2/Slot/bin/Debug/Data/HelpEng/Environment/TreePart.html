Description

In Tree Action creates all program stuffing your program. All objects , events , steps , conditions , cycles , comments are in Tree Action

Structure tree next action:

most first indigenous branches are Windows . In them, a branch of the windows, put all objects of the window. Then come events for each object. And they are all action , conditions and cycles . Comments can be located anywhere in the tree action.

You can freely move, copy, paste, delete all the elements of the tree action. In addition, you can edit of two ways:
1) Select the editable action and change it in the text box , which is just below the tree action.
2 ) Either right-clicking on the Effects of lead are editing the menu and select Edit (or just double-click on the action). And Action section will be displayed and ready to edit the selected action.

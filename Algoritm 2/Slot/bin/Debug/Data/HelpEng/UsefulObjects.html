Description

Useful sites (data, files and folders, registry) are working as ordinary objects , but do not have a graphical interface and are used for interaction Operating System Windows (open file, find the date and time, the coordinates of the cursor)

Useful objects strongly expand your opportunities. Using them can reach the most remote corners of the operating system (eg change desktop wallpaper ). And also they facilitate certain calculations (eg calculation dates ).

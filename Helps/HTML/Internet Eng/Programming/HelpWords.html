Description

Quick fill words are letter symbols for specifying of different values (colors, cursors, etc.). They are used when it is necessary to assign a the property the value.

For example, to make the window red it is enough to assign property Back color a quick fill word "Red". Or if it is necessary to set a specific Text Align on a button, then you should assign the property Text Align a quick fill word "Center".

Quick fill words change nothing and do nothing, they are just information specified in objects' properties. All quick fill words are grouped in categories (paths, image align, cursors) and located in menu, which becomes available after clicking with right mouse button on any text field in Development Environment of Algorithm2.

Colors There all basic colors are collected. By equating one of quick fill words of this group (red, blue, yellow, etc.) with some color property (Back color, Fore color) you'll color the object in a way you need.
